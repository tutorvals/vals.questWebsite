# Vals' Activity menu

Welcome to a bit of my life! Here I list some activities I like, in case that makes it easier for us to schedule a hang out or find common interests :)

- **Conversation**, on whatever you're enthusiastic about, on topics from the [conversation menu](/conversations)
  - Eg. what's on your mind, personal projects going on, hopes and dreams, becoming oneself, understanding the world
  - Phenomenology - what is it like to be you, what's the basis and direction of your life
- **Helping out** on whatever work or project you're currently doing, debugging or brainstorming or handiwork (please let me build your ikea furniture).
- **Exploring physical space**, urban or rural (ie. walking around, potentially disregarding barriers)
  - Hiking and camping and swimming in lakes and rivers
- **Sports**
  - Movement: hiking, climbing, running, cycling, swimming, parapente, skiing, yoga/stretching, acrobranche/via ferrata, dance
  - 1v1 or team game: badminton, spear fencing, ball games, martial arts eg. Brazilian jiujitsu and wrestling, ping pong
- **Social dancing** (lindyhop, rock and roll 6 time, blues, fusion, contact improv)
- **Board games**
  - Social deduction: Blood on the Clocktower, OneNightWerewolves, SecretHitler, ...
  - Strategy eg. Dominion, Clank, MTG, Root, ...
  - I'll try anything once :D
- **Roleplaying** (Shadowrun, Ars Magica, C'thulu, Shadow Musketeers, free form improv, ...) — [see RPG page](/rpg)
- **Math/logic/coding/physics/philosophy puzzles**
- **Arts**
  - Theatre, musicals, films
  - Listening to and playing music, singing
  - Musical playing and improv, jamming - I play piano, and basic guitar
  - Sharing on books, reading literature or poetry for others
  - Experimenting with creating stuff
- **Video games**
  - Multiplayer: shooters (CS, Overwatch), RTS (AoM, Rise of Nations, AoE4), platform fighters (Brawlhalla, Rivals of Aether 2), various (7 Days to Die, Minecraft)
  - Solo adventures: survival exploration (eg. Subnautica), puzzle games (Understand, Baba is You)
- **Writing**
- **Meditation**
- **Massages, cuddling**
- And I wanna try new things! Share stuff you like or would try :D

## Less liked activities (things I'm generally not into)

I'm able to do any of these, but they're not my favorite for regular activity.

- **High effort food** (higher end restaurants, or 30min+/person home cooking) — I like usual food a lot already
- **Loud noise environments** (crowded bars) — I enjoy dancing during parties - music isn't noise when I'm dancing but is when I'm talking to people
- **High risk & high danger things** (eg. high free solo)
- **Doing the same activity too much** — I get satiated. Maybe a few days for skiing or hiking, an hour for puzzles, many hours for cuddling or conversation
